6. Structured Products

Suitable only if they add convexity rather than harvest yield.

Acceptable structures: uncapped participation certificates, LEAPS-equivalent call structures, long-dated warrants/calls, or principal-at-risk thematic notes with no autocall, no worst-of, no income objective, no low cap, and transparent downside.

Required termsheet fields: underlying basket, tenor, participation, cap, downside formula, principal protection, barriers, autocall terms, worst-of terms, issuer risk, secondary liquidity, bid schedule, and rationale.

Preferred underlying baskets: equal-weight quantum basket, space/satellite/launch basket, AI-bio basket, nuclear/power/grid basket, or single-name structures on high-conviction dislocations. Avoid mega-cap tech baskets for this mandate.

Rejected: autocallables, reverse convertibles, income notes, worst-of notes, low-cap buffered notes, opaque downside barriers, and any structure where the bank owns the convexity while the investor keeps crash risk.
