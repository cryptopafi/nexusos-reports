Tier 1 — Metabolic reprogramming (the lab-matched lever)

Retatrutide — Bachmeyer-endorsed; research-status (NOT FDA-approved)

  • Indication (lab-linked): HOMA-IR 16.21 + insulin 56.6 + suspected MASLD.
  • Live-NLM evidence: “Retatrutide monotherapy improves HOMA-IR by up to 68% over 6 months” and “reduces systemic inflammatory markers including CRP, IL-6 and TNF-α by 47% in 12 weeks” [NLM: Extended].
  • Independent web confirmation (2024): In the phase-2 MASLD substudy (Nature Medicine 2024, NCT04881760), retatrutide cut HOMA2-IR by up to ~69% and fasting insulin by up to 70.9% at 48 wk; liver fat fell up to 86% at 12 mg, with liver fat <5% achieved in 89–93% at the 8–12 mg doses; no hepatotoxicity signal through 48 wk [WEB: Sanyal et al., Nat Med 2024; PatientCareOnline 2024]. This is among the largest liver-fat treatment effects yet reported.
  • Discrete dose / titration: DATA NOT FOUND (live NLM) — the notebooks cite outcomes but no mg/week starting dose, escalation step, or ceiling (3 narrow queries to both notebooks failed to surface a numeric titration). Trial-derived dose range (informational, not a prescription): 1–12 mg SC weekly with slow escalation; benefits dose-dependent. Final dose must be set by the prescribing physician.
  • Evidence tier: ⚠️ Research-status / not FDA-approved (phase-3 TRIUMPH + SYNERGY-Outcomes ongoing, NCT07165028). Romanian access only via trial enrollment or gray-zone compounding (acknowledged quality/legal risk).
  • Standard-of-care alternative (FDA-approved, Romania-accessible): Tirzepatide (Mounjaro/Zepbound) — dual GIP/GLP-1, ADA-preferred for diabetes+obesity with weight-loss efficacy; SURPASS-3 substudy showed liver-fat −8.1% over 52 wk with ALT/AST/GGT reductions [WEB: ADA SoC 2026; JCTH 2024]. Bachmeyer regards it as under-delivering on insulin resistance, but it is the defensible second-best if retatrutide is inaccessible.
