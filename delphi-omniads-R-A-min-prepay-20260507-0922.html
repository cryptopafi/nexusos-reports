6. VOX Backend — Strategic Signal

VIVA Bolivia + VOX Solutions Partnership September 2, 2025

VOX Solutions was named exclusive international A2P (Application-to-Person) SMS and Voice gateway for VIVA Bolivia. The VOX-360 platform handles anti-fraud capabilities including flash call authentication and A2P SMS monetization for Viva's entire network. This is one of Bolivia's 3 major carriers (Tigo, Entel, Viva).

Implication for Omni Ads: Omni Ads campaigns routing to Viva Bolivia subscribers go through the same VOX backend infrastructure. This creates potential preferential termination rates vs. third-party API providers quoting $0.22/msg, and the architecture for negotiating direct rates.

7. Verdict — Recommended Per-GEO Floors

NO-GO: Uniform $50 Global Floor
Ecuador: $50 buys <150 messages, campaigns fail, SMBs churn. Bolivia: $50 is too high if VOX-native routing achieves $0.03–0.05/msg (kills acquisition at the top of funnel). Romania: $50 is adequate but not differentiated from competition.

Competitive Positioning
The market has two SMB entry tiers: (A) No-minimum PAYG (Twilio, Bird, Vonage) with high per-message LATAM rates creating economic barriers, and (B) $49/month subscriptions (Sinch Engage, ProTexting) with recurring friction. Omni Ads hybrid prepay wallet is structurally superior — one-time prepay, no subscription, credits don't expire. Differentiated per-GEO floors with transparent messaging ("Your $50 sends X messages in Romania") creates the right SMB expectation and reduces first-campaign failure rates.
