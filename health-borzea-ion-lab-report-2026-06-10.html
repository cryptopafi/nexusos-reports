Protocol decision

No peptide protocol is recommended or required from this panel alone. The current evidence supports a clinician-review conversation around cardiometabolic risk context, especially LDL-C, fasting/insulin context, HbA1c/glucose context, medications, and current peptide/supplement exposure.

What would be needed before protocol work

  • Original lab reference ranges and confirmation of units for HbA1c, HDL-C, and insulin rows.
  • Full lipid context: ApoB, Lp(a), triglycerides, non-HDL-C, blood pressure, waist/weight trend, family history.
  • Current medications, supplements, peptide use, TRT status, GLP-1 history, fasting status, and symptoms.
  • Clinician review before any medication, peptide, supplement, injection, dose, cycle, or stack change.
