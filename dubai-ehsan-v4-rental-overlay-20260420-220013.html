✓ RENTAL NOI OFFSETS

Net yields 3.4-4.1%. 4-year cumulative NOI AED 10.0-10.7M portfolio. Rental income meaningfully rescues total return where capital gain disappoints.

The Verdict

Lunaria at AED 33M is the anchor. It is the only one of the three priced near community fair value (+8% premium) and the only asset producing positive capital gain in the realistic base case. Rental NOI of AED 4.47-4.67M over 4 years is a genuine floor.

Chorisia 1 at AED 15M and Nest at AED 22M are income plays, not flips. Both were acquired at material premiums to community fair value; capital recovery requires luxury-premium maintenance through 2030. Rental yield is strong (3.65-4.07%), but they cannot individually hit the 100% flip mandate.

Recommendation: Single-pick Lunaria at AED 31M (counter), or diversified basket of Lunaria + Chorisia 1 to blend capital anchor with the highest net yield. Accept that the 100% mandate is no longer the frame — pivot to risk-adjusted total return.

⚠ HIGH FLAG — Al Barari HOA unconfirmed

No evidence Al Barari community management rules explicitly permit DTCM holiday home registration. Gated luxury communities in Dubai increasingly restrict STR. Must verify directly with Al Barari developer before committing to STR thesis.

Even if permitted: Al Barari inland location (~25 min from Downtown/beach) suppresses STR demand vs Palm Jumeirah. Chorisia 1 base STR case = 5.1% net (at 60% occupancy), marginal vs 4.0% LT net. Bear case (50% occupancy) = 2.0% net — worse than LT. STR upside does NOT justify HOA risk.

§7 · Total Return Matrix — Capital Gain + 4y Net Rental

Total Return = (Net Exit − Mortgage − 4y Interest − 2% Exit Fees) + 4y cumulative NOI midpoint − Cash In. Financial model: 50% LTV, 4.5% IO, 7% entry fees, 2% exit fees. Ehsan's current offer prices.

Two exit-value views — reality lies between
Method A — Premium Preserved: Exit = Entry × (1+CAGR)^4. Assumes property retains its premium-to-community positioning.
Method B — Mean Reversion: Exit = BUA × (community avg AED/sqft × (1+CAGR)^4). Premium erodes to community average.

For a 3-asset portfolio generating AED 3.5M+ gross rental, LLC structure = ~AED 280K+ annual CIT drag. Only justifiable if non-tax benefits (succession, liability firewall) exceed the tax cost.

Conclusion

Individual direct ownership dominates. All NOI projections in this report assume individual ownership and zero personal tax. If legal/succession considerations push toward LLC, re-model net yields down ~8% to account for CIT.
