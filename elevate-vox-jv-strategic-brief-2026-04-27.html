Executive Summary

VOX is proposing a three-party Joint Venture (JV) — Pafi + Leo + a VOX-side party (the parent VOX Solutions Global Ltd, its VOX Ads division, or Ehsan Ahmadi personally), capped at 33% — to handle all affiliate marketing separate from Elevate. VOX provides capital and people. Pre-negotiated KPIs (Key Performance Indicators) trigger acquisition; consideration is paid as stock options in VOX Solutions Global Ltd, a private Hong Kong company.

The structural improvement of the 33% cap is real — founders combined hold 67%, controlling ordinary resolutions. But VOX retains blocking minority rights on every special resolution (mergers, dilution, IPO, charter changes), plus operational squeeze power via the existing Framework Agreement (Annex 1 §2.2 lets VOX raise SMS+ rates unilaterally, crushing AffCo margin and missing KPIs).

With the right protections — make-whole clauses, cross-default triggers, tag-along, anti-dilution, parent guarantee, and external M&A (Mergers & Acquisitions) counsel — this can be a €2-10M+ outcome per founder. Without them, it is a slow-acquisition trap.

$300M VOX Ads target = our biggest leverage. Aggressive growth implies VOX has capital to deploy and a board willing to pay strategic premiums for tuck-in acquisitions. AffCo at $10-20M revenue = 3-7% of their target — small enough to swallow, big enough to matter. Anchor every conversation against this number.

VOX's likely strategic motive (steelmanning their side)

  • Vertical de-risking: Affiliate gambling/finance is messy compliance-wise. Spinning into AffCo keeps the VOX brand clean.
  • Captive demand flywheel: VOX captures both sides — SMS+ delivery margin AND affiliate-network margin.
  • Talent capture: Pafi (network connections, tech stack) + Leo (strategy, market access). Cheaper than hiring + retaining.
  • Compliance firewall: AffCo holds the licenses. Regulator hits AffCo, parent stays insulated.
  • Preempt competition: Smsads.net + Elevate could pivot to a competing aggregator. JV kills that optionality.
  • EBITDA dressing: Low cash + earn-out + stock structure has minimal cash impact for VOX but reportable revenue accretion.
  • Reach $300M target: AffCo at $10-20M revenue = 3-7% of next-year target — material, achievable, justifies acquisition spend.

KPI Tiers — 3-Tier Earn-Out Ratchet

Industry-standard structure: Floor / Target / Stretch. Hit any 3 of 5 KPIs at any month-end after month 18 → triggers the corresponding tier acquisition. Multi-window measurement gives us breathing room and prevents single-quarter sandbagging.

Valuation Formula

Don't fix a number — fix a formula tied to KPI overshoot. Industry-standard for earn-outs.

Acquisition Price = max( R × Net Revenue (TTM),
                         M × EBITDA (TTM) )

Tier        R     M     Multiplier rationale
─────────  ───   ───    ──────────────────────────────────────────
Floor      2.5×   8×    Pure-financial affiliate baseline
                        (Awin/CJ comparable)
Target     3.5×  11×    Strategic-captive premium
Stretch    5.0×  14×    Strategic + retention + growth premium

Why max(R × Rev, M × EBITDA): protects us against VOX margin-squeeze. If they raise SMS+ rates and crush EBITDA, the revenue floor still holds.

Make-Whole clause is mandatory: if VOX takes any action that mathematically prevents KPI achievement (rate hike beyond X%, market exit, blocking access, withdrawing operational support), the relevant KPI is deemed met. Without this, VOX can squeeze us at the precise moment our earn-out measures.

The Stock-Option Trap

VOX Solutions Global Ltd is a private Hong Kong company. Stock options in a private foreign company carry these realities:

RealitiesConsequence
No liquidity / no public marketYou can sell only when VOX is acquired, IPOs (no announced plan), or buys back voluntarily
Valuation is set by VOXNo 409A in HK, no independent appraiser. VOX picks the number.
Standard 4-year vest with 1-year cliffIf VOX terminates "for cause" (vague definition) at month 11 → zero shares vested
Drag-along riskIf VOX sells parent at low multiple (1.2× revenue), we drag at the same multiple
Tax double-hitHK options to RO/IE-resident individuals can trigger taxation at grant, vest, exercise, AND sale — 30-55% leakage possible
Foreign currency riskHKD-denominated, 5-10 year horizon, FX swings unpredictable
Information asymmetry foreverMinority option-holder in private foreign co may never see audited financials post-acquisition
