v4.3 Calibration Patches (recommended before next wave)

The Wave 3 scan surfaced three structural issues in the v4.2 framework that warrant a v4.3 patch:

  1. Pessimistic CAGR floor must be segment-specific. Current 5% floor + 0.90 sale haircut yields uniform ROE_pess between −17% and −28% across the entire branded/trophy universe. Patch:
  2. Al Barari Villa: floor 6.5% (DLD-implied 8%)
  3. Palm Jumeirah Signature: floor 6%
  4. Palm Jumeirah Branded (One Palm, Tiara): floor 7.5%
  5. Emaar Beachfront branded: floor 7%
  6. Dubai Hills Maple: floor 6%

  7. Off-plan markets (Oasis, PJA): keep 4–5%

  8. Off-plan ROE math should account for payment-plan leverage. Address The Bay 10/80/10 plan = 10× equity leverage during build-out. v4.2 treats P_close as full cash outlay, which understates IRR by ~3–4×. Patch: add payment_plan field to listing input, compute weighted-equity over hold period.

  9. Tier C and D-only renovation formulas trap profit. Wave 3 Nest analysis showed Tier D+ext PASSES while Tier C/D-only FAIL — because B/C/D-only compound P_close, but D+ext compounds full project cost basis. This creates a counterintuitive "extension > pure reno" gate. Patch: align all reno-path formulas to compound (P_close + reno_cost + ext_cost) consistently.
