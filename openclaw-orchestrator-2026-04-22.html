Critic Pass — Top-3 Risk Flags

gpt-oss-120b — Risk: OpenRouter provider routing variance
TTFT ranges 0.27s (Clarifai) to 0.79s (benchLM). OpenRouter auto-routes to cheapest provider. If Clarifai/Baseten aren't the routed backend, latency could degrade. Mitigation: pin provider via OR's provider.order param or test via X-OpenRouter-Provider header. Still well under 3s even at worst-case.
gpt-4o-mini (backup) — Risk: TTFT at ceiling
Measured 1.6–3.2s TTFT. At P95 load, can breach 3s target. For richpro dispatching to sub-agents via sessions_spawn, this accumulates per dispatch call. Acceptable as backup but not primary. Had reported 30% failure rate in parallel tool_calls (fixed by strict=true JSON schema).
gpt-oss-120b — Risk: Rate limits on OpenRouter
Newer/less-popular model on OR. Rate limits may be lower than GPT-4o-mini. At 50 sessions/day the call volume is low (50 RPM bursts max), unlikely to hit OR rate limits. Monitor first 48h after deployment.
