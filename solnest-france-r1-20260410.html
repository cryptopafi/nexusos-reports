Q3 — ADEME TREMPLIN Invalidation Phantom

D4 PHANTOM PROGRAM CONFIRMED. D4 cited "ADEME TREMPLIN" as a core France cleantech grant. This program does not exist as described for SolNest.

Program A — ADEME Tremplin pour la transition écologique des PME

  • What it is: PME ecological transition grant from Plan de Relance. Funds physical operations: energy audits, waste management equipment, ecodesign studies. Pre-listed catalogue of 60 eligible actions.
  • Status 2026: Stated closure Dec 31, 2025. As of April 2026, likely exhausted or closed. First-come first-served.
  • Grant range: €5K minimum to €200K maximum.
  • SolNest eligibility: Ineligible Program requires physical/operational ecological investments. Pre-revenue SaaS does not qualify.

Program B — French Tech Tremplin (Bpifrance)

  • What it is: Bpifrance acceleration for founders from underrepresented backgrounds (Edition 5). Mentoring + incubation + access to broader Bpifrance stack.
  • Status 2026: ACTIVE. BIC Montpellier lists "French Tech Tremplin 2026 - Volet Incubation."
  • SolNest eligibility: Conditional Depends on founder profile (underrepresented criteria). Does not provide cleantech grants directly.

Verdict: Neither matching program applies. D4 score impact: remove approximately 1–1.5 points from France 8.6 score.

Sources: agirpourlatransition.ademe.fr, cress.corsica (citing ADEME), paysdelaloire.cci.fr, bpifrance.fr Tremplin Edition 5, actinitiative.org

Q4 — French SAS Substance Requirements Critical Gap

Hard fact (primary source lafrenchtech.gouv.fr): "Companies must meet these ownership restrictions: not more than 75% of capital held by foreign entities." A Delaware C-Corp owning 100% of a French SAS is blocked from French Tech 2030.

For other programs (Bpifrance standard grants, Bourse French Tech):

  • Local director: Not explicitly required. SAS needs a président (can be foreign).
  • Office address: French registered address required for incorporation — domiciliation services qualify.
  • Minimum employees: No minimum headcount in early-stage grant requirements.
  • Founder residency: NOT explicitly required. French Tech Visa available but not mandatory.
  • Banking: French bank account required for disbursement (operational, not substance).
  • Case study: ZERO documented examples of Delaware-owned French SAS accessing or being rejected from French Tech grants.

Assessment: The lack of documentation is itself the risk signal. The 75% cap on French Tech 2030 shows the French system is aware of foreign ownership as a factor. Whether Bourse French Tech has a similar unpublished rule is unknown and cannot be assumed safe.

Sources: lafrenchtech.gouv.fr (75% cap), tresor.economie.gouv.fr

Final Verdict NEED MORE DATA

Why not GO France immediately

The accessible France stack has collapsed from ~€700K (D4 implied) to ~€200K (R1 confirmed), and even that €50K grant (Bourse French Tech) has unverified foreign ownership eligibility. Incorporating a French SAS before confirming Bpifrance's position on 100% Delaware parents risks €3-5K incorporation cost + 4-6 weeks for a program that may reject the application.

Why not GO Finland instead (yet)

Finland Sprint Grant has a confirmed August 2026 deadline and no foreign ownership cap identified. However, the full Finland stack was not verified in this R1 task. A Finland R1 equivalent would be needed before commitment.

Why not GO both

No revenue, no employees, one founder. Two-entity structure adds legal cost (~€15-20K), accounting complexity, and substance requirements in both jurisdictions. Premature at pre-MVP stage.

What specific data is needed (priority order)

  1. [BLOCKING] Email/phone query to Bpifrance: "Does a French SAS whose sole shareholder is a Delaware C-Corp (100% foreign ownership) qualify for Bourse French Tech?" — Answer expected within 5 business days.

  3. [MEDIUM PRIORITY] Verify Finland Sprint Grant full eligibility: nationality restrictions, incorporation timeline, and what other Business Finland programs exist in 2026 post-Tempo discontinuation.
  4. [MEDIUM PRIORITY] Check if any ADEME program actually serves pre-revenue SaaS cleantech startups (the Agir pour la Transition catalogue has 50+ programs — targeted scan needed).
