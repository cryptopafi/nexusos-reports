CRITICAL FINDING — Pessimistic gate is binary fail across community

The v4.2 pessimistic formula (P_sale_pess = P_close × 1.215 × 0.90) yields a uniform −17.5% ROE_p regardless of asset, because at 5% CAGR the compounded sale price barely exceeds 1.17× P_close (the cost stack: 1.0 purchase + 0.06 entry + 0.11 4yr interest). This means: if Al Barari forward CAGR mean-reverts to 5%, NO turnkey resale clears equity costs at any close discount in {0–30%}. The ROE_b column is the realistic case (8% forward), the ROE_p column is the stress test that says “if the macro thesis breaks, the entire community loses money on equity, regardless of which unit you buy”. Hold-vs-flip framing matters — at 5% CAGR you must HOLD past 4 years for the compounding to outrun cost stack, OR the renovation arbitrage path (separate math, see V-cards) becomes the only flip-positive path.
