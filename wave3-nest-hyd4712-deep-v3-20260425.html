4. Structural Finding — The Formula Scissors

Under v4.2 math, the ROE landscape has a discontinuity:
  • Tier B / C / D reno P_sale = P_close × growth × premium — reno cost adds to equity denominator without compensating sale uplift. The more you spend, the worse the ROE.
  • Tier D + extension uses total project cost basis × growth × 1.18 premium — reno+ext spend compounds INSIDE the sale price, generating outsized P_sale (~AED 65M).
The math says: don't half-renovate this villa. Either turnkey-flip cheap (Tier B) or fully extend (Tier D+ext). Tier C and D-only are structural traps.
