§7 — Blended Strategy Recommendation

Chorisia 1 — LT + STR option

Best gross yield ratio. Manageable SC absolute. IF Al Barari HOA permits STR: consider STR Year 1–2 then switch to LT ahead of 2030 exit. STR base case = 5.1% net vs LT 4.03% net. STR bear case = 2.0% net — worse than LT.

Nest — Pure Long-Term

7,462 sqft is difficult STR product. LT tenant provides stable 3.74% net. Clean vacant possession at 2030 exit optimises capital appreciation realisation. Do NOT pursue STR.

Lunaria — Pure LT / Capital Play

14,118 sqft is extremely niche LT AND STR. Accept 3.32% net yield. Lunaria is a capital appreciation thesis (acquired near community avg), not an income asset. 4yr NOI = ~AED 4.4M; 2030 exit at base = AED 37.2M vs AED 33M cost.

§9 — Best Practices + Yield Optimization

Confirmed Yield Killers

1. SC drag: AED 7.57/sqft/yr non-negotiable. Lunaria = AED 106,873/yr. Largest recurring cost after mgmt.
2. Large unit size: 14,118 sqft Lunaria attracts UHNWI who negotiate hard + take 4–6 months to find. Void risk severe.
3. Location: Al Barari = Dubailand, ~25 min from beach. Suppresses STR tourist demand vs Palm/Marina.
4. RERA renewal caps: 29% rent gap observed. Must secure exit from existing tenants to reset to market.
5. Furnishing capex: AED 300K–1.5M not captured in gross yield headline. Amortise over 5 years.
