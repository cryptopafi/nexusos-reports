Position 1

HPA reboot mandatory before GH peptides

Flatlined HPA axis prevents pituitary response to Tesamorelin or other GH secretagogues. GH pulses remain weak. Oral DHEA alone does not address neurological root cause. Reset is peptide-driven, centered on DSIP and supported by Selank/Semax for neuroplasticity.

Position 2

Methylation via MOTS-c, not oral cofactors alone

Methylated B-complex required even with normal B12/folate. Specifically endorses Riboflavin 80 mg twice daily. TMG and P5P dosing not specified in his corpus. Direct folate cycle optimization (clearing homocysteine) relies on MOTS-c 5 mg SubQ every other day.

Position 3

Enclomiphene + Kisspeptin-10 concurrent, NOT HCG

Strongly opposes HCG (burns out Leydig cells, receptor desensitization). First-line while preserving fertility:

  • • Kisspeptin-10 100–500 mcg SubQ Mon/Wed/Fri, max 8–10 weeks per cycle.
  • • Enclomiphene oral, dose titrated by bloodwork.
  • • Run concurrently, not sequentially.
Position 4

LAD plaque: KPV + BPC-157 + TB-500 + MOTS-c

  • • KPV 100–500 mcg SubQ daily — prevents foam cell formation, 11% CAC reduction, 35% MACE reduction at 12 weeks.
  • • BPC-157 500 mcg–1 mg SubQ daily — endothelial patching + NO restoration.
  • • TB-500 5 mg SubQ every 5 days — reverses myocardial fibrosis.
  • • MOTS-c 5 mg SubQ every other day — mitochondrial biogenesis + folate cycle.

Peak injection count

~5–6 SubQ per day at the busiest (Monday AM through Friday PM).

Injection site rotation

Rotate systematically abdomen → flanks → upper thighs to avoid lipohypertrophy and irritation. AOD-9604 is the exception: always at thoracic paraspinal near T10 Schmorl node for local lipolytic action.

Syringe mixing rules

  • • GHK-Cu never mixes with any other peptide. Copper ion oxidizes and destroys them.
  • • Healing peptides (BPC-157, TB-500) do NOT mix with GH secretagogues (Tesamorelin). Different pH and stability profiles.
  • • Kisspeptin-10 and MOTS-c both AM SubQ: separate syringes, different sites.

Reconstitution and shelf life

Reconstitute with 1–2 mL bacteriostatic water slowly down the side of the vial. Swirl gently. Never shake. Store reconstituted vials refrigerated in the dark. Stable significantly longer than the commonly quoted 28-day rule per Bachmeyer's clinical experience; protocol-level safety is 28–30 days.
