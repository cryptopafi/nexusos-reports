Off-Plan Complication (Flagged)

UAE mortgages are typically only available post-handover. For PJA + Oasis assets, the analysis assumes Model (a): staged developer payment plan during construction (20% down + 30% construction-linked), mortgage activates at handover (~2028), effective interest period = 2 years of the 4-year hold.

If Model (b) were assumed instead (50% LTV as if turnkey), interest would be overstated by ~2 years and ROE calculations would understate the return. The cash-in profile is also spread across construction rather than upfront, which improves IRR (not reflected in ROE figure).
