D4. Stack-ability Analysis

Core rule: Each EU subsidiary receiving aid from its own member state is a SEPARATE legal entity. Different entities receiving aid for activities in their respective countries = NOT double-dipping. The EU SME consolidation test (Rec. 2003/361/EC) applies per subsidiary: Delaware parent with 0 employees/€0 revenue → each EU sub qualifies independently as EU micro-enterprise.
