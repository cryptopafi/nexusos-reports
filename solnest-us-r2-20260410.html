Q6. Solar Prize Ready! — Tactical Guidance

Ready! stage requirements (from Round 8 Rules PDF)

  • 90-second video describing the solution
  • 2-page Technical Assistance Request (TAR) describing technical challenges (not a proof of concept)
  • No prototype, no MVP, no revenue required
  • Concept-only entry: eligible and historically successful at Ready! stage

Time investment for a Ready! entry

  • Video: 1-2 weeks (scripting, recording, editing)
  • TAR: 3-5 days
  • Total: approximately 2-4 weeks for a quality submission
Key framing requirement. The entry must articulate a specific US solar industry challenge being addressed. "AI rooftop marketplace" must be framed as solving a US solar deployment bottleneck (customer acquisition, installer matching, cost reduction) — not just described as a SaaS product.

Set! stage PoC requirement: design a proof of concept plus demonstrate market demand. Irrelevant for SolNest in its current configuration due to the eligibility block.
